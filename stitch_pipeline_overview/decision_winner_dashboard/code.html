Strategic Trade-offs

While **Protocol B** achieved the highest peak F1 score on the validation set, its poor calibration and high variance across random seeds suggested an over-fitting to specific syntactic structures of the ViHSD dataset. **Protocol C** was selected despite a 0.5% lower F1 because of its significantly more robust handling of tone-marked sarcasm and its production-ready latency profile of <15ms per inference call.
